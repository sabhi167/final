Dual degree
programs

Dual Degree Program is a unique program from Amrita University for the students to get an international degree and an Indian degree together. Amrita University has tied up with leading universities in USA, Netherlands, Sweden, France and Italy in various disciplines like MBA, MTech, MS, and MSc to provide the students with the skill and exposure of international universities along with Amrita’s curriculum.

Amrita Chancellor's scholarship

Scholarships helps students with merit to achieve their dreams. Amrita University is a part of the Indian Government scholarships and, the University also has Chancellor’s Scholarship program, which has a waiver up to 100% in tuition fees. Moreover, the University has scholarship schemes for stipends to cover living expenses and/or health benefits expenses, etc. The amounts depend on the scheme, the academic program, location, etc.
